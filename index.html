Pricing Information

€50

Essential Driver Training (EDT) Lessons

I charge €50 per hour for each EDT lesson commencing in Tipperary Town. A programme of 12 EDT lessons has to be completed before a learner will be given a date for the driving test.

€50

Non EDT Lessons

Generally these are lessons taken in preparation for the driving test. I will charge you €50 per hour for all such lessons. These prices also apply for BE lessons i.e. someone doing the test for a vehicle with horse / cow box in tow.

€80

Car Rental for Driving Test

This is the amount I charge for the use of my car for your driving test.
